The Halfling is one of the 10 races avaliable for play. It can be unlocked by killing special enemies, which sometimes will drop trophies. If playing with the Adventurer class, the trophy will have a 100% chance of unlocking any class & race, which may be the Halfling or any other race not already unlocked. If playing with another class than the Adventurer, the trophy will have a 50% chance of levelling that class up, and a 50% chance of unlocking a new class & race.

Racial bonus: Guts (+1 to max armour when you kill at least 15 skulls in one attack)

Mortal enemy: Invisible (Has a very high defense value, but low health. Easily taken care of with poison)

Mortal enemy effect: Other skulls appear with double armour

Unlocked with class: Raider
