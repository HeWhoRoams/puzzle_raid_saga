The Adventurer is one of the 10 races avaliable for play. When playing with it, killed Special skulls will sometimes will drop trophies, which will have a 100% chance of unlocking any class & race. If playing with another class, the trophy will have a 50% chance of levelling that class up, and a 50% chance of unlocking a new class & race.

The Adventurer is the first class avaliable, and comes with the race Human. It has no special characteristics (other than the potential to select from more than twenty abilities in one game) and cannot be customized in any way.

Class perk: None

Class flaw: None

Class skill: Teleport (Get completely new tiles, which will not contain any skulls)
